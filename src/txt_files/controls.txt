MOUSE:
LEFT CLICK (+ LSHIFT): add ADD # of logos randomly on the screen (show info on new)
MIDDLE CLICK (+ LSHIFT): remove ADD # of newest logos on the screen (remove at mouse)
RIGHT CLICK (on logo) [+ LSHIFT] {+ LCTRL}: add ADD # of logos at the cursors location (show info) [show info on new]
{Shows info on all}

ScrollUP (+ LSHIFT) (+ R/G/B) [+ LCTRL]: add 1 to the ADD counter (add 10 to the ADD/R/G/B/base counter) (R: change red)
(G: change green) (B change blue) [changes the base]
ScrollDown (+ LSHIFT) (+ R/G/B) [+ LCTRL]: remove 1 from the ADD counter (remove 10 from ADD/R/G/B/base counter) (same as above)
[same as above]

KEYBOARD:
F5: remove all.
F1: refresh

ESC: exit
PAUSEBREAK: main menu

F2: take screenshot

F3: show/remove all options
T: show total
A: show add
H: show leader
M: show avg hits
S: show total hits
F4: show RGB
F6: cycle bg color

all of the following are per frame:
(i would almost never use these)
(just use the mouse wheel instead
unless you're trying to get in the 
thousands)
PAGEUP: add 5 to the ADD counter
PAGEDOWN: remove 5 from the ADD counter
HOME: add 10 to the ADD counter
END: remove 10 from the ADD counter
