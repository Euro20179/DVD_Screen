MOUSE:
LEFT CLICK (+ LSHIFT): add ADD # of logos randomly on the screen (show info on it/them)
MIDDLE CLICK (+ LSHIFT): remove ADD # of logos randomly on the screen (remove logo at mouse)
RIGHT CLICK (on logo) [+ LSHIFT]: add ADD # of logos at the cursors location (show info) [show info on new]

ScrollUP (+ LSHIFT): add 1 to the ADD counter (add 10 to the ADD counter)
ScrollDown (+ LSHIFT): remove 1 from the ADD counter (remove 10 from the ADD counter)

KEYBOARD:
all of the following are per frame:
UP: add .1 to the ADD counter (yes .1 because 1 is VERY FAST)
DOWN: remove .1 from the ADD counter
PAGEUP: add 1 to the ADD counter
PAGEDOWN: remove 1 from the ADD counter
HOME: add 5 to the ADD counter
END: remove 5 from the ADD counter
F5: remove all.

ESC: close window

PAUSEBREAK: take screenshot

F1 (+ C)show info (show controls)
T: show total
A: show ADD
H: show leader
M: show avg hits
